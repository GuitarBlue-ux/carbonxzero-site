The problem

Industrial decarbonization needs faster materials innovation.

Heavy industry remains one of the hardest sectors to decarbonize. Materials development cycles are often slow, fragmented, and difficult to scale.
~30%
of global CO₂ emissions are associated with heavy industry and industrial systems.
  • Traditional discovery-to-deployment cycles can span years to decades.
  • Industrial materials systems are capital-intensive, complex, and highly path-dependent.
  • Scalable low-carbon alternatives must satisfy both technical performance and manufacturability.
